About Beats By Maps

Our Story

Beats By Maps started in a small bedroom with a broken mirror and makeup as a form of escape. It grew from pain, healing, and self-expression into something meaningful and creative. “Beats” represents transformation and confidence, while “Maps” represents the founder, Maipelo, and her journey. Today, it stands as proof that beauty can be created even from difficult seasons.

Mission

To help clients look and feel their best through creative and professional makeup artistry.
