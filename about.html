About Beats By Maps

Our Story

Beats By Maps was never supposed to become a business. It started in a tiny bedroom with a broken mirror, cheap makeup brushes, and a girl trying to distract herself from how heavy life had become. There were nights when silence felt louder than anything else. Nights filled with overthinking, disappointment, and the feeling of not being enough. Makeup became the only thing that could quiet the noise for a little while. At first, it was never about beauty. It was survival. Every brush stroke became a way to hide tired eyes from crying. Every layer of foundation covered stress no one else could see. While the world kept moving, makeup became a small space where pain could pause for a moment. The name “Beats By Maps” came from the idea that every face tells a story — every scar, every insecurity, every smile forced through difficult days. “Beats” represented transformation, confidence, and art. “Maps” represented the journey behind every person who sits in the chair. There were days when giving up felt easier. Clients were few. Money was tight. Support was inconsistent. Sometimes it felt embarrassing to dream so big while struggling silently behind the scenes. But somehow, every time someone looked in the mirror after an appointment and smiled differently — smiled like they finally saw themselves as beautiful — it reminded her why she started. Beats By Maps was built from pain, long nights, self-doubt, and healing. It became more than makeup. It became proof that something beautiful can still come from broken seasons.

Mission

To help clients look and feel their best through creative and professional makeup artistry.
